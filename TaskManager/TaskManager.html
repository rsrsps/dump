Click anywhere in this box to dismiss.

Sandbox is a place for ideas that might spontaneously come to mind.
Tasks maintains a list of things to do, how long they'll take, and when to do them by.
Review maintains a list of learnt items to review at exponentially increasing intervals.

???: opens this help box.

+name description: adds an entry in sandbox with the given name and description.

-name: removes the sandbox entry corresponding to name.

\task duration deadline description: adds a task named task, expected to take duration amount of time and be done by deadline. deadline can be expressed absolutely (e.g. 12/11/2016, 12:14AM) or relatively (e.g. 1d2h30m4s -> due in 1 day, 2 hours, 30 minutes, and 4 seconds).

/task: remove task from list.

~prompt answer lifespan: adds an item to review. Program will prompt you with prompt and expect answer when lifespan amount of time has passed. If answered correctly, lifespan will double and the counter will be reset; else, lifespan is halved.

prompt: force program to review the item corresponding to the prompt prompt before its lifespan has fully elapsed.
