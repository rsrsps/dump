U T Pi S AS L H O (EPLL)

2-generator reduction

2GR is an advanced speedsolving method that quickly reduces the cube to the <R,U> subgroup, leading to a very ergonomic F2L step and an 84-algorithm 1-look LL.

Substeps in outline:

EOPair: orient all the edges and create a 2x1x1 corner-edge pair at DBL-DL
CPLine: complete a 3x1x1 line on LD while reducing the remaining 6 corners to <R,U>
Block: expand the 3x1x1 line LD into a 3x2x2 block
F2L: complete the F2L
2GLL: complete the last layer in 1 look
